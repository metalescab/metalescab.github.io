The Balseiro Institute has published a tribute to Manfred Ahlers, a pioneer and great teacher of our current Metals Physics Division.
The tribute consists of an article by Mgter. Laura García, which is available at the following link:
[https://www.ib.edu.ar/comunicacion-y-prensa/noticias/item/2206-nota-manfred-ahlers.html](https://www.ib.edu.ar/comunicacion-y-prensa/noticias/item/2206-nota-manfred-ahlers.html)

The article includes details about Manfred's life, memories from various people who knew him, and reflections on his legacy as a teacher at the Balseiro Institute and a researcher at the Bariloche Atomic Center. Additionally, we share a couple of notes published on the occasion of Manfred's passing, with words from Marcos Sade, Francisco Lovey, and Armando Guillermet.
